Music Is Joy

Human Life is a brilliant synthesis of Ras (Emotion/Mood), Rang (Colour) and Roop (Form).
Music is present as the expression and in the experience of life.
It is a phenomenon far beyond the limited realm of entertainment.
It is much so embedded and integral to our life and the universe that we live in.
Try to hear the sound of your own breath by closing your ears with your palms.
Our breathing in and breathing out, both have a different shruti or scale of sound.
Our heart beats to a certain rhythm that swings up and down as per our emotional frequencies.
Music of the birds can gently wake us up and a soft lullaby can put us to deep sleep.
While the bhangra beats can make a non-dancer swing, the melancholic shehnai can swing the mood to tears.
We seldom realize that we still remember the multiplication tables due to the tune.

Music & Us

The central element in the system of Music is the Raaga.
Raaga is a sanskrit word defined as an act of providing a colour or an emotion through a combination of notes.
Our ancient scripts have documented the emotions associated with the various Raagas as love, laughter, anger, compassion, disgust, horror, heroic, wonder, peace and spiritual devotion.
Our Life is an active expression of all these emotions.
Music gives an alignment to our thoughts, restores peace of mind and thereby generates self-confidence and containment.
Music, if adopted in its true essence can trigger and sustain wellness into our mundane islandic lives.
For our elderly, who live isolated lives and suffer diverse physical & mental illnesses, music contains definite solution to restore healing.
For our children, it can enable their learning process and help them imbibe human values.
For the youth, it can channelize emotions and kindle a sense of collaboration & harmony.
